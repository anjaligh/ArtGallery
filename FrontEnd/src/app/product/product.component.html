About the Artwork

Abstract painting inspired by a beautiful dream. As in a dream, in abstract painting the natural scientific laws are completely or partially abandoned, there is neither consistent perspective nor space, light or shadow, large or small - the impossible can become possible. Wonderful Dreamland is a modern, relaxed, detailed and lively color composition in orange, yellow, blue, white,magenta, pink, green, violet, lilac, beige and brown colors in front of a flat background. The painting invites you to dream...

The painting is stretched on a 3.8 cm deep gallery frame and can be hung without an additional frame, as the picture sides are also painted
